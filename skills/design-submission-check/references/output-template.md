# Submission Audit Output Template

Use the user's language. Lead with the decision and unresolved Blockers.

## Final decision

```text
Decision: Ready | Conditionally ready | Not ready
Audit coverage: Full | Partial
Target: {award / cycle / track / category / stage}
Deadline: {absolute date and official timezone}
Blockers: {count} | Important: {count} | Optimization: {count}
```

Add one sentence explaining the controlling reason. State whether portal upload and payment were checked.

## Findings by priority

| ID | Severity | Requirement | Affected item(s) | Finding and evidence | Required action | Recheck method |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Order by Blocker, Important, then Optimization. Within each level, place fastest submission-critical fixes first.

## Requirement checklist

| Requirement ID | Material or field | Constraint | Observed | Status | Official source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Use only `Pass`, `Fail`, `Not checked`, and `Not applicable`.

## Cross-material consistency

| Canonical fact or claim | Expected value | Conflicting location | Observed value | Resolution |
|---|---|---|---|---|

If no contradiction is found, say which material types were actually compared; do not claim universal consistency.

## Rights and disclosures

| Asset / content | Source or owner | Basis / proof | Disclosure needed | Status | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Use `Cleared`, `Pending`, `Unknown`, or `Restricted`. Add: `Risk screening only; not legal clearance.`

## Final upload set

| Order | Final filename | Requirement ID | Version | Checksum, if available | Ready? |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

List excluded drafts and duplicates separately so they cannot be uploaded accidentally.

## User-executed final actions

Include only actions the skill could not perform, such as portal preview, account permission, payment, declaration acceptance, final upload, confirmation email, or physical delivery.

## Official sources

List direct current official links with `checked on: YYYY-MM-DD`. Separate controlling rules from supplementary FAQ or organizer clarification.
